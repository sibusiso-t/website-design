About Us

Ketlababalla Mashele (Pty) Ltd was established in 2014 as a unique HR Consultancy Firm. The company emerged in order to empower and professionalize new, old employees and youth that are aspiring to climb up the corporate ladder through various professional avenues such as performance coaching, mentorship, shadowing and leadership training initiatives. It comprises of qualified and experienced professionals that have been in leadership and management positions as well as in Human Resources for a period longer than 25 years. Our knowledge of South African Labour market, regulations, trends and practices enables us to tailor make services according to the needs of our customers in their respective industries. This is coupled with proven benchmarked tools and methodologies that enable lost lasting and effective decision making where people are concerned.
